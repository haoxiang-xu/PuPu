You may do the following without separate written approval, as long as you do
not imply endorsement, sponsorship, affiliation, or official status:

- refer to the Project by name in factual statements;
- say that your software is "based on PuPu", "forked from PuPu", or

- link to the official repository or official releases; and
- redistribute unmodified official builds while preserving existing notices.

You must obtain prior written permission before you:

- use "PuPu" as the name of a modified product, fork, hosted service, or

- register or use confusingly similar product names, domains, package names,
  social accounts, or app listings; or
- imply that your product, company, service, or distribution is official,
  endorsed by, or affiliated with the Maintainer.

## 5. Rules for forks and modified distributions

If you distribute a modified version of the Project, you must:

- remove or replace PuPu trademarks from the app name, icon, splash screen,
  store listing, website, and other primary branding surfaces; and
- clearly state that your version is an unofficial fork or modified build.

You may use a factual statement such as "Forked from PuPu" in descriptive text.
